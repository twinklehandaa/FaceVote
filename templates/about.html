About FaceVote

Our Mission

FaceVote is dedicated to enhancing the integrity and accessibility of campus elections through the implementation of cutting-edge facial recognition technology. We believe in fostering a democratic environment where every student's voice can be heard securely and without the risk of fraud.

Our Team

FaceVote is developed by a team of passionate students and faculty dedicated to innovation and civic engagement. We bring expertise in software development, cybersecurity, and election management to create a robust and reliable voting platform.
