Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is FaceVote?

FaceVote is a secure and reliable online voting system for campus elections that uses facial recognition for voter authentication.

How does FaceVote ensure election security?

We employ advanced facial recognition technology to verify voters, preventing fraudulent activities and ensuring only eligible students can cast their ballots.

Registration

How do I register my face for voting?

On the registration page, you will be prompted to upload a clear photo of your face. Our system will securely store a unique digital representation of your facial features.

What if I have trouble registering my face?

Please ensure you are in a well-lit environment and the photo clearly shows your face. If you continue to experience issues, please contact our support team.

Voting

How do I cast my vote?

Once registration is open, log in to the voting platform. When you are ready to vote, you will be asked to verify your identity via facial recognition before you can access the ballot.

Is my vote anonymous?

Yes, while facial recognition is used for authentication, your actual vote is recorded anonymously and cannot be linked back to your identity.
